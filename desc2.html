Caffe Macchiato

Savor the distinct flavor of our Caffè Macchiato, a bold espresso delicately "stained" with a touch of frothy steamed milk.
This delightful contrast of strong coffee and a hint of creamy goodness creates a captivating taste that’s both invigorating and smooth.
Perfect for those who appreciate the pure essence of espresso with just a whisper of indulgence.
